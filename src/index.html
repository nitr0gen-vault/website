Nitr0gen Vault

A new type of wallet security


With Nitr0gen you have full control and access over your keys without the worry of losing them. Your crypto keys do not exist at any single location at rest or transit. On your device you have generated a OTK (One time key) which gives you control and access. The OTK can be lost and you will still be able to access your crypto keys once authenticated.
